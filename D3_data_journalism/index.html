Correlations Discovered Between Smoking and Age

The correlation in the state of UT shows the lowest percentage of smokers and age range. The state of West Virginia has the the highest age and percentage of smokers. The bulk of the states are clustered around age 36-40 years where the percentage of smokers is between 14%-20%. There are seven (7) states (LA, MS, IN, AR, KY, TN, WV) where the percentage of smokers is above 22% and the age range is between 36-42 years.
